    What is Hica Prmitr ?

    HICA (Higher Institute Campus Ambassador) is a dynamic and forward-thinking initiative at Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology & Research (PRMITR), Badnera, dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. As a student-driven tech club, HICA serves as a platform for aspiring innovators, engineers, and technology enthusiasts to collaborate, experiment, and expand their horizons beyond traditional classroom learning.
